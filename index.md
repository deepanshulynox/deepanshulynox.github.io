## 💡 Overview

Welcome to **project-status-reports**! This application helps you generate project status reports quickly and easily. It uses AI to automate the process by leveraging the power of **n8n**, **Gemini API**, and various project management tools like **Asana**, **ClickUp**, and **Jira**. Whether you are managing a small team or leading a large project, this tool streamlines your reporting tasks and enhances communication with your team.
